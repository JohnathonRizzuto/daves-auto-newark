About the shop

The guy your buddy keeps recommending.

Dave's Auto opened in 1994 and never moved off Fennel Road. We do everything from a normal oil change to a full classic-car restoration — same shop, same crew, same honest writeup at the front desk.

Brakes, suspension, exhaust, engine work, diagnostics, electrical, A/C, transmission. If the check engine light is on, we'll tell you what it actually means before we touch anything.

Most repairs come with the NAPA AutoCare 24-month, 24,000-mile nationwide warranty — same coverage you'd get at a dealership shop, without the dealership prices.
